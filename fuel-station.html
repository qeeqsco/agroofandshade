Fuel Station Manufacturing

We are Fuel Station Canopy Manufacturer in Kerala and Also Deal In PAN India. We Manufacture and Assemble the material for Fuel Station Canopy and dispatch it to the site location in PAN India. Fuel Station Canopy Fabrication or Fuel Station Canopy Manufacturing is done as per the design & drawing approved by the company or dealer of the Fuel Station. The Designing of Fuel Station Canopy as per Standard IS Code with verification of all forces & loads which is applicable on a Fuel Station Canopy. We have our Self Product Unit & Man-Power for the Fuel Station Canopy manufacturing. We are Manufacturing Fuel Station Canopy for Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited, (HPCL), and Jio BP, we are Providing our Canopies for other Company Like Hindustan Bio Diesel, Indizel MEE, Nayara, etc. The Specific material of the Fuel Station Canopy is as per the Make list of the company or decided by the company parameters.

We also provide the Fuel Station canopy Fascia with the complete accessories like Fascia LOGO, Light, Column Spreader, Tags, and Small Canopies for Watercolor, Air Machine, DG set, We provide you all services of fuel station in a single platform. We can also provide you with a separate part of the canopy like – Fuel Station Canopy Structure, Bare Galv. Roofing, Water Gutter, False Ceiling, Dommey Fascia, LED Light fittings, Cables, Fascia RVI work, Column cladding, Spreader, logo, etc.

Including Civil Work of the Fuel Stations are done as per the company parameters and client instructions and it will be completely supervised by our respective engineer.

We are also winning substitutional works from other countries like Dubai, Senegal, Uganda, South Africa, China, and Latin America.

    Designing & Drafting of Fuel Station Canopy

    Designing Of Canopy – Canopy is designed as per Indian National Building Code and Other Relevant IS Code for buildings. Live Loads & Wind Loads are taken as per IS 875:1987, and Seismic. Allowable stress in steel and concrete is taken as per IS 800: 1984 and 2000 respectively.

    The Canopy is Pre – Engineered at Factory

    The Canopy is Pre – Engineered at Factory : – Canopy Primary & Secondary Members is to be Fabricated at Factory with all necessary material and bolting as per drawing. Canopy material Warranty & Stability against the work is our Key services, we provide our best and try to maximize this for a particular time

    Pre – Fabrication of Canopy

    Pre – Fabrication of Canopy – The Primary and Secondary parts of the Canopy are Pre-Fabricated in factory space, where we can construct it inaccurate size & Maintain the quality of work. All material is taken as SAIL/JSW/TATA/ISI. etc.

    Installation or Erection of Pre-Fab Canopy

    Installation or Erection of Pre-Fab Canopy – Once the Canopy structure is fabricated as per the drawing, Canopy each part is assembled on-site for final erection. Before erection, the Pre-Engineered canopy, and the final water level of the column and bolt have to check & after the erection, we should also check again and checkout also whole structure nut-bolt is properly tightened.

    Installation of Secondary Material/Accessary

    1. The Bare Galvanized Roofing sheets erection done with water gutter & drainage pipeline.
    2. Once the Roofing is sheeted, We start the False Ceiling. It is a metal – 150F false ceiling with off-white powder coating material approx. 30 – 45 micron.
    3. In parallel to false ceiling work, light fitting & cabling are continuing. Also, some other work that goes inside the false ceiling is done.

    Complete PEB Canopy Erection

    The complete Canopy erection over then the canopy Back Fascia sheet covers the width of the canopy. Here is the 0.50 mm – 0.91mm GI sheet used for cladding the canopy width. The back fascia is used to stop inserting the wind into the canopy and protect from any kind of damage

    Fascia RVI Work

    Canopy Fascia RVI's work shows the Branding of the Corporation and its differential to Other stations. The 3mm Aluminum Composite Penal is used for Canopy Branding Work. There will be acrylic design over the facia according to the brand.
