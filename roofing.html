Roofing

We are engaged in providing Roof sheet installation services. These services are rendered by our dexterous team of professionals using sophisticated technology to meet the specifications of patrons and international quality standards. Further, we make use of self-treading screws to ensure easy fixing and long-life functioning. The offered services are highly praised by the customers for their timely execution and reliability.

  • Waterproof The roof sheets installed by us are waterproof to save the buildings from rain.
  • Heat insulation helps in lowering the temperature in summers.
  • Frost-resistance Our sheets can withstand many cycles of thawing and freezing without disintegrating.
  • Long service time Sheets designed using best grade materials ensure longer service life.
  • Easy to install The range of sheets is easy to install and needs less maintenance.
  • Resistant to wind Owing to optimum design, these sheets are ensures complete resistance to wind.
